Field Training

Give Your Dog a Solid Foundation

Training a hunting dog can be a long, tiring, and even frustrating process for someone that lacks the experience. Not to mention, you could be doing more harm than good if you are using training tactics that are not best suited for your dog.

Keith has been training many different kinds of dogs for decades, but discovered his love for the pointing field setter about eight years ago. Since then, he has become highly skilled in the art of training and hunting with Llewellin Setters in the field. His track record speaks volumes of his ability, with all of his dogs possessing their Junior titles, his oldest its Senior Hunt Master title and his second oldest on its way to Senior.
